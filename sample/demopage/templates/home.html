Top 5 Common Skin Diseases and Their Causes

Acne

Cause: Excess oil production, clogged pores, bacteria.

Eczema

Cause: Allergies, immune system dysfunction.

Psoriasis

Cause: Immune system problem leading to skin cell turnover.

Rosacea

Cause: Inflammation of the skin, genetic factors.

Melanoma

Cause: Excessive UV radiation exposure, genetic factors.
